About CropMinds

Team CropMinds consists of the four of us Mohammed Salman Hussain, Mohammed Kareemullah, Ayaan Tabrez and Rayyan Shareef. We are the students of 2nd year AIDS-B. The project SmartSow is an initiative taken by Team CropMinds to help and aid the modern day farmers in terms of Crop Recommendation, Fertilizer Recommendation, Farmers Market, Disease Detection and Yield Detection.

Project SmartSow

Farmers face challenges like limited crop care guidance, pest control issues, water and fertilizer management difficulties, unpredictable yields, and complex market access.

There is a lack of an integrated data-driven digital platform that provides personalized crop management, pest and disease identification, irrigation and fertilizer tips, and economic planning tools

This is where Project SmartSow enters , filling up the need by developing an Integrated Digital Solution for Precision Farming:
Empowering Farmers with Data-Driven Crop Management, Economic Planning, and Growth Tracking to Enhance Yield, Profitability, and Sustainability.

With features like Crop Recommendation, Fertilizer Recommendation, Farmers Market, Disease Detection and Yield Detection, this project revolutionizes the modern day farming.
